This competition project for a primary school in Reykjavik is situated on a peninsula near the former industrial area of Vogabyggð. The island, intended to be bike-accessible, has an 8-meter height difference between the neighbourhood and the school site. The design solves this problem by integrating the bike route with the school, creating functional connection.
