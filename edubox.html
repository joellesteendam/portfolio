The EDUbox provides a learning environment for underserved communities and refugee camps which will offer the inhabitants of these communities the opportunity to develop themselves and start a better life.

Because the EDUbox is designed within the ISO defined requirements, it can be shipped as a normal shipping container to places in need. It is efficent in transport and installing and flexible to adapt to different education programs.

The project has been built as a 1:1 prototype and was showcased at the Dutch Design Week 2022. Following this, the Edubox was transported to a refugee camp in Jordan for its initial testing phase.
