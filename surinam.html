About

In the final year of high school, a senior home was designed, commissioned for the housing project ‘De Schuilplaats’, located in Suriname. ‘De Schuilplaats’ is a foundation which builds high quality houses in old-Surinamese style for an affordable price with the aim of reducing the housing shortage in Suriname. The design of the senior home is based on the traditional Surinamese house style but with a cost price of 25.000 USD. Surinamese people live mostly outside. This is why the senior house has a double roof, so that one of the terraces will always be in the wind, which provides the necessary cooling in the Surinamese tropical climate. All windows and doors are placed opposite to each other, so that optimal use can be made of the almost always present Suriname wind to keep the house as cool as possible.
