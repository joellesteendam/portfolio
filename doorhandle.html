The Grotiushuis building, officially named Westblaak II, located on Westblaak in Rotterdam, was designed by Herman Bakker in 1958 and is now a municipal monument. Though still functioning as an office building, it required a new entrance. De Zwarte Hond was tasked with redesigning the original entrance of the Grotiushuis, also necessitating a new door handle. To find a fitting door handle for the new entrance, sketches, digital visualizations, and full-scale 1:1 models were created to test the design and ergonomics. Additionally, the door handle had to accommodate necessary technical equipment, ensuring both aesthetic and functional integration.
